TabList Help

TabList is an extension which helps you to view and manage your tabs.

What can it do?

  • main feature — show list of all opened tabs, also from other windows if this option is switched on in settings;
  • follow all tabs activities — when tab is getting opened, closed, moved or refreshed, TabList will track changes and update list accordingly;
  • show bookmarks bar if switched on in settings;
  • open tab (focus on it) — click on favicon;
  • close tab — click close;
  • reload tab — click refresh;
  • discard tab (free memory) — click memory;
  • move tab — click, hold and drag drag_handle.
Don't hesitate to report issues or ask features requests!
