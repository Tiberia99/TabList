TabList Help

TabList is an extension which helps you to view and manage your tabs.

What can it do?

  • of course, show list of all current tabs, also from other windows (you can switch this option in settings), but not private;
  • follow all tabs activities — when tab opens, closes, moves or refreshes, it will track it and update list accordingly;
  • show bookmarks bar (you can switch this option in settings). Also you can click on link to open it;
  • open tab (focus on it) — click on favicon;
  • close tab — click ×;
  • reload tab — click ⭮;
  • discard tab (free memory) — click ❄.
Don't hesitate to report issues or ask features requests!
